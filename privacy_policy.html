Privacy Policy for Afnan Quran

Effective Date: July 12, 2026

Afnan Quran ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Afnan Quran — أفنان القرآن mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

a. Location Information

The App requests access to your device's location (GPS/Network-based) solely to calculate accurate, local prayer times (Salah times) and to determine the direction of the Qibla (Mecca).
Note: This location data is processed entirely on your local device. We never transmit, upload, share, or store your location data on any external servers or with third parties.

b. Audio Storage Access

The App may access your local storage to cache Quran recitations or prayer audios. This access is local to your device and is not shared.

c. Personally Identifiable Information (PII)

We do not collect, store, or share any Personally Identifiable Information such as your name, email address, phone number, device identifiers, or contacts.

2. Third-Party Services and Analytics

Our App does not use any third-party advertising networks, user tracking systems, or external analytics SDKs that collect user data. The App is 100% ad-free.

3. Security

Since the App operates locally on your device and does not collect or transmit personal information over the internet, your user data is inherently secure within your device's sandboxed environment.

4. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information and is completely safe for use by children. We comply with all COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) regulations.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to contact us at your support email address.
